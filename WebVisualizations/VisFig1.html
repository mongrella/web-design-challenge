Max Temperature

Max temperature peaks at around 20 degrees latitude and then steeply drops as latitude increases in the Northern Hemisphere. The relationship is non-linear and there is a slight positive relationship, then a negative relationship after 20 degrees latitude. The Southern hemisphere has higher max temperatures.
